About FRAME

Leading the digital transformation of fiscal compliance in Zimbabwe.

FRAME (Fiscal Revenue Automated Management E-Solution) is a leading provider of comprehensive fiscalisation and Fiscal Data Management (FDM) compliance solutions in Zimbabwe. The company specializes in virtual fiscalisation, helping businesses streamline financial processes, reduce operational costs, and enhance efficiency while ensuring full compliance with local tax regulations. In 2015, Scentpro Tech signed up as Regional Implementation and Support Partner for Fiscal Revenue Solutions (FRS). FRAME was developed six years later in response to ZIMRA requirements, backed by a technical team with extensive experience in fiscal device installations, support, maintenance, and compliance.

  • ZIMRA-compliant virtual fiscalisation. Stay tax compliant with a solution that meets all Zimbabwe Revenue Authority requirements.
  • Cloud-based and hardware-free. No physical devices needed, making setup fast and cost-effective.
  • Customisable in-house software. FRAME can be adapted to your POS, ERP, or accounting system.
  • Real-time fiscal reporting. Instantly monitor your transactions and performance from anywhere.
  • Offline functionality. Keep operating and reporting during internet or power outages.
  • Scales with your business. Multi-branch and multi-level integration across locations and platforms.
  • Data security and local support. Built with enterprise-grade security and responsive local support.

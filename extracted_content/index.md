About FRAME

Leading the digital transformation of fiscal compliance in Zimbabwe

FRAME (Fiscal Revenue Automated Management E-Solution) is a leading provider of comprehensive fiscalisation and Fiscal Data Management (FDM) compliance solutions in Zimbabwe. The company specializes in virtual fiscalisation, helping businesses streamline financial processes, reduce operational costs, and enhance efficiency while ensuring full compliance with local tax regulations.

In 2015, Scentpro Tech signed up as Regional Implementation and Support Partner for Fiscal Revenue Solutions (FRS). FRAME was developed 6 years later in response to ZIMRA requirements. Our technical team has extensive experience in fiscal device installations, support, maintenance, and compliance.

Our Mission

To empower businesses in Zimbabwe through innovative and affordable virtual fiscal solutions that ensure seamless compliance with ZIMRA regulations.

Our Vision

To become Zimbabwe's leading provider of digital fiscal compliance solutions, driving a future where businesses thrive through simple, smart, and fully virtual technologies.

Our Services

Comprehensive fiscalisation solutions for your business

Seamless Integration

Tailored for seamless integration with existing business systems such as POS or ERP platforms.

ZIMRA Compliance

Direct integration with the Zimbabwe Revenue Authority (ZIMRA) for regulatory compliance.

Remote Management

Customizable dashboards for remote access and device management.

Custom Development

Built in-house, FRAME  is fully customizable to match your business processes, system integrations, and industry needs.

Ongoing Support

Continuous monitoring and local support to ensure smooth operations.

Referral Program

Join our network of independent resellers and earn commission.

What Makes Us Different

Innovative solutions designed for your business needs

ZIMRA-Compliant Virtual Fiscalisation – Stay tax compliant with a solution that meets all Zimbabwe Revenue Authority requirements

Cloud-Based & Hardware-Free – No physical devices needed, making setup fast and cost-effective

Customizable In-House Software – We built FRAME ourselves, so we can adapt it to your POS, ERP, or accounting system

Real-Time Fiscal Reporting – Instantly monitor your transactions and performance from anywhere

Offline Functionality – Keep operating and reporting during internet or power outages

Affordable Pricing – One-time installation fee of $50.

Multi-Branch & Multi-Level Integration – Scales with your business across locations and platforms

Data Security & Local Support – Built with enterprise-grade security and responsive local support
